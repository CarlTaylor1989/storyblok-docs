The management api is rate limited to 3 requests per second for users on the free plan and 6 requests per second for other users.

;examplearea

| Plan | Rate Limit |
|------|-----------|
| Free | 3 per second |
| Basic | 6 per second |
| Advanced | 6 per second |
| Premium | 6 per second |
| Enterprise | Custom |
